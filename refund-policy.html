Refund Policy

This Refund Policy explains how DN Physics handles refund requests for premium access and digital purchases.

Premium access is a digital product. If a user has actively used the service for more than 3 days after purchase, the purchase is generally considered consumed and a refund will normally not be valid, unless required by law or approved at our discretion.

1. Digital Product Nature

DN Physics provides digital educational content, premium access, and account-linked features. Because access may begin immediately after purchase, refund eligibility can be limited.

2. Refund Request Window

Refund requests should be submitted as early as possible after purchase. Requests made after meaningful account use, premium access consumption, or extended usage may not qualify.

3. 3-Day Usage Rule

If the purchased service has been used for more than 3 days after the purchase date, refund eligibility is generally not available, except where legally required or in rare exceptional cases approved by the platform owner.

4. Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds are typically not granted for completed digital access periods, consumed learning access, accidental purchases caused by user error, or requests made after extended usage.

5. Exceptional Cases

We may review refund requests in exceptional situations such as duplicate payment, verified technical billing error, or payment processing issue.
