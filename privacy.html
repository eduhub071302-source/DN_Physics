Privacy Policy

DN Physics respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected across the DN Physics website and app experience.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect information necessary to provide account access, learning progress, support, and payment-related services. This may include your name, email address, account details, study progress, and certain usage information.

2. How We Use Information

Your information is used to operate the platform, provide premium access, improve the learning experience, synchronize progress across devices, provide customer support, maintain service quality, and improve platform performance.

3. Payments

Payments are processed securely through approved third-party payment providers such as PayHere. DN Physics does not directly store full card details or full payment instrument information on its own servers.

4. Learning Progress and Account Data

If you create an account, your study progress, selected preferences, and certain usage data may be stored so your experience can continue across devices and future sessions.

5. Guest Mode

If you continue as a guest, some data may remain only on your current device and may be lost if browser storage, app storage, or device data is cleared.

6. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information with trusted third-party service providers only as necessary to support authentication, hosting, database services, analytics, technical infrastructure, and payment processing, or where required by law.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, misuse, loss, or disclosure. However, no internet-based service can guarantee absolute security.

8. Cookies and Similar Technologies

DN Physics may use cookies, local storage, and similar technologies to maintain sessions, remember preferences, improve performance, and enhance user experience.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of DN Physics after updates are published means you accept the revised version of this policy.
