Privacy Policy

DN Physics respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected across the DN Physics website and app experience.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect limited information required to provide account access, learning progress, support, and payment-related services. This may include your name, email address, account details, and app usage information.

2. How We Use Information

Your information is used only to operate the platform, improve learning experience, synchronize progress across devices, provide support, maintain service quality, and improve app performance and reliability.

3. Payments

Payments are processed securely through approved third-party payment providers such as PayHere. DN Physics does not directly store your card details or payment instrument information on this site.

4. Learning Progress & Account Data

If you create an account, your study progress, selected preferences, and certain usage data may be stored so your experience can continue smoothly across devices and sessions.

5. Guest Mode

If you continue as a guest, some data may remain only on your current device and may be lost if browser storage, app storage, or device data is cleared.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational steps to protect your data from unauthorized access, misuse, loss, or disclosure. However, no internet-based service can guarantee absolute security.

7. Third-Party Services

DN Physics may rely on trusted third-party tools and infrastructure providers for authentication, database services, hosting, analytics, and payment processing. These services may process data only as needed to deliver platform functionality.

9. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of DN Physics after updates means you accept the revised version of this policy.
